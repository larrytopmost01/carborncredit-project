Climate Credit Network

The Climate Credit Network is an enterprise division of SMEFUNDS - a Social Enterprise organization providing low cost, clean and safe Technology (Lighting & Cook Stoves) for low income households. The organization creatively use Social Marketing to economically empower her Green Ambassadors through Carbon Credit Earnings thereby lifting them out of poverty and fighting climate change in Africa and other low income economies. Climate Credit Network (CCN) is the renewable energy advocacy movement that has been responsible for the training, support and success of a network of professionals who are on a mission to promote, educate and distribute commendable and innovative renewable energy Solar & Biofuel solutions to the residential markets across Africa. The name Carbon Credit is derived from the concept developed by the "United Nations Kyoto Protocol which is targeted at making easier access to renewable energy cheap and free for developing nations through the Clean Development Mechanism" and it begins with YOU. Carbon Credit is the generic term for any Tradable Certificate or Permit representing the right to emit one tonne of carbon dioxide or the mass of another greenhouse gas with a carbon dioxide equivalent (tCO2e) equivalent to one tonne of carbon dioxide.

Carbon Credits and Carbon markets are a component of national and international attempts to mitigate the growth in concentrations of greenhouse gasses (GHGs). One carbon credit is equal to one metric tonne of carbon dioxide, or in some markets, carbon dioxide equivalent gases. Carbon trading is an application of an emission trading approach. Greenhouse gas emissions are capped and then markets are used to allocate the emissions among the group of regulated sources.

Our goal as a network is to help every member of this organisation own a renewable energy portfolio, become energy independence, help you reduce your electricity bills and promote a green economy while ensuring that we help you offset the amount of carbon you generate and eventually make handsome royalty income sharing some of our innovative products that just helps you reduce the amount of carbons you emit. Vision of the Climate Credit Network

Our vision is to create a strong, democratic, grass-roots movement to bring renewable energy to the masses and improve social, economic and environmental conditions in Africa.

Missions of the Climate Credit Network

At Climate Credit Network, we have a threefold mission:

  • Product Mission: To promote environmental friendly products
  • Economic Mission: To help people start up a Green Business
  • Social Mission: To create awareness and empower people for change

Product Mission

We are providing a solution for every member within our network to produce their own renewable energy, save money, and create a greener and more secure world for our children.

Economic Mission

We operate a sustainable business-both economically and ecologically-that adds value to the lives of our Independent Green Ambassadors and our customers.

Social Mission

We want to give power back to the people through technology and education, improving the security of our country, the quality of our life, and the purity of our environment-locally, nationally & globally.

The Goal of Our Network

Our goal is to provide low cost, clean and safe Energy (Lighting & Cook Stoves) for low income households. We use Social Marketing to economically empower her independent distributors through Carbon Credit Earnings thereby lifting them out of poverty and fighting Climate Change in Africa.
